# User Guide

Welcome to the Atito Civil Academy LMS! This guide provides comprehensive instructions for students to navigate the platform, enroll in courses, and master new skills.

### 1. Getting Started
#### Registration
1.  Click the **"Register"** button on the top navigation bar.
2.  Enter your **Full Name**, **Email Address**, and a secure **Password**.
3.  Click **"Create Account"**. You will be automatically logged in upon success.

#### Login
1.  Click **"Login"** on the top navigation bar.
2.  Enter your registered email and password.
3.  Click **"Sign In"** to access your dashboard.

### 2. Dashboard Overview
Your **Dashboard** is your central hub. Here you can find:
-   **My Courses**: A list of all active courses you are enrolled in.
-   **Progress Bars**: Visual indicators showing your completion percentage for each course.
-   **Recent Activity**: Quick links to jump back into your last watched lesson.

### 3. Course Discovery & Enrollment
1.  Navigate to the **"Courses"** page via the main menu.
2.  **Filter** courses by category (e.g., *Construction*, *CAD*, *BIM*) using the sidebar or dropdown.
3.  Click **"View Course"** on any course card to see the full syllabus, instructor bio, and learning outcomes.
4.  Click the **"Enroll Now"** button. The course will immediately appear in your Dashboard.

### 4. The Learning Experience
#### Video Player
-   **Play/Pause**: Click the video or use the spacebar.
-   **Navigation**: Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons to move between lessons.
-   **Auto-Complete**: Lessons are automatically marked as "Completed" when the video finishes.
-   **Manual Complete**: You can also click the **"Mark as Complete"** button if you've watched it previously.

#### Course Sidebar
-   The sidebar displays the entire course curriculum.
-   **Green Checkmarks** indicate completed lessons.
-   Click any lesson title to jump directly to that topic.

### 5. Assessments & Quizzes
Some courses include quizzes to validate your knowledge.
1.  **Module Quizzes**:
    -   You must score **70% or higher** to unlock the next module.
    -   Modules are locked sequentially. You cannot skip ahead.
2.  **Final Exam**:
    -   Available after completing all modules.
    -   You must score **70% or higher** to pass the course and unlock your certificate.
3.  Click **"Submit Quiz"** to see your score instantly.
    -   *Note: You can retake quizzes to improve your score.*

### 6. Certificates
Upon successfully **passing the final exam** with a score of 70% or higher, you can download your certificate of completion.

**Important**: Certificates are only awarded for passing the final exam, NOT for completing individual modules.
1.  Complete all course modules and pass their quizzes (70%+).
2.  Take and pass the **Final Exam** with a score of 70% or higher.
3.  After passing, a **"View Certificate"** button will appear on the results page.
4.  Click the button to view and download your certificate as a PDF.
5.  Your certificate can also be accessed from your Dashboard for completed courses.

## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting Guide

### Login Issues
-   **"Invalid Email or Password"**: Double-check for typos. Ensure Caps Lock is off.
-   **Account Disappeared?**: Since this is a demo version using **LocalStorage**, clearing your browser cache or using "Incognito Mode" will wipe your account data. You will need to re-register.

### Video Playback
-   **Video Won't Play**: Check your internet connection.
-   **"Video Format Not Supported"**: Ensure you are using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
-   **Broken Links**: Some demo videos are placeholders. If a video fails to load, click "Next" to proceed to the next lesson.

### Progress Tracking
-   **Progress Not Saving**:
    -   Ensure **Cookies** and **LocalStorage** are enabled in your browser settings.
    -   Do not use "Private/Incognito" windows if you want your progress to persist after closing the browser.
